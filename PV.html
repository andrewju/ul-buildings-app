Plassey Village

Home Location Like

Plassey Village is located across the road from the main entrance to the University of Limerick. Plassey Village is a great place to make friends, especially in first year, because most of the houses sleep 8 people. There are also 4-bedroom houses, which are ideal for postgraduate and senior students. Terraced houses are grouped around landscaped courtyards, which helps to create a cozy, communal atmosphere. Shops are located very close to the village, and a regular bus service to Limerick operates every 20 minutes from a stop directly opposite the village.
