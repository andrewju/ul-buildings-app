PESS Building

Home Location Like

Home to the Department of Physical Education and Sport Sciences (PESS), the PESS Building was first constructed in 1972 and significantly upgraded in 2012. With an area of 6,350m2, the PESS Building holds 6 teaching spaces, including the 150-seat lecture theatre P1-033, and a multi-function hall, sports hall and gymnasium. The Right Track café sits in the entrance foyer of the building.
