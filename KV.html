Kilmurry Village

Home Location Like

Kilmurry Village is a complex of terraced houses in landscaped grounds, close to the Sports Arena and to all UL sports facilities, including tennis courts and playing fields. The village is a great place to stay if you want to be on the track or in the pool before breakfast. Houses sleep 6 to 8 students and have spacious living room/kitchen facilities.
