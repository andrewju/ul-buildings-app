The UL Sports Pavilion building was completed in 2011. Located on the north campus on the site of UL's 9.8-hectare all-weather pitches sports complex, the 1,810m2, 2-storey Pavilion includes changing rooms, referee rooms, administration offices and a restaurant and is surrounded by 4 raised floodlit sports pitches with associated boundary fencing and car and coach parking.
