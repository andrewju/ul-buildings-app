Analog Devices Building

Home Location Like

The Analog Devices Building was completed in summer 2015. With an area of 7,459m2, the building is a state-of-the-art research facility that makes up part of the Bernal Institute. The building houses departments from the Faculty of Science and Engineering. It contains offices and teaching facilities, including seminar rooms and a 200-capacity lecture hall.
