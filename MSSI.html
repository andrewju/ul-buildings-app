MSSI Building

The MSSI Building was completed in 2002. With an area of 2,900m2, the building contains laboratories attached to the Materials and Surface Science Institute, which is a department of the Faculty of Science and Engineering. The building was extended in 2015 and now forms part of the Bernal Institute.
