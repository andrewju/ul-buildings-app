Red Raisins Cafeteria

Located in the Main Building, Red Raisin is the home of Subway, Starbucks and Pizza Pronto. In addition to those 3 outlets, sandwiches and confectionery are available elsewhere in the cafeteria during the day.

Red Raisin Cafeteria opening hours: Monday to Thursday 9.00 to 16.00, Friday 9.00 to 15.00
