Languages Building

Home Location Like

The Languages Building was completed in 2008. With an area of 1,645m2, the 3-storey building includes teaching and language laboratory facilities and offices and is home to the School of Languages, Literature, Culture and Communication; the UL Language Centre; and Aonad na Gaeilge, the UL Irish Language Centre.
