Cappavilla Village

Home Location Like

Cappavilla Village was completed in 2006 by Project Architects. With an area of 15,058m2, the student village has 500 bedrooms in 2-, 4- and 6-bedroom apartments. The village is constructed on the highest point of the campus and commands excellent views of the River Shannon and surrounding countryside. The village has a launderette, games room and enclosed bicycle area.
