The Engineering Research Building and Millstream Courtyard Building were completed in 2005 by Murray O'Laoire Architects. The area of the Engineering Research Building is 3,905m2 while that of the Millstream Courtyard Building is 1,371m2. The main discipline in the Millstream Courtyard Building is languages.
