UL Boathouse

Home Location Like

The UL Boathouse was completed in 2007 by John Thompson & Partners. Located on the bank of the River Shannon near Thomond Village and with an area of 1,875m2, the boathouse provides storage, training and shower facilities for the sub-aqua, mountain bike, kayaking and rowing clubs. A main feature of the building is the indoor rowing tank, which accommodates up to 8 rowers. The water flow rate can be set to mimic river flow conditions, from calm to choppy and everything in between.
