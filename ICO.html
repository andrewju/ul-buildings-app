Irish Chamber Orchestra Building

Home Location Like

The Irish Chamber Orchestra Building was completed in 2008. With an area of 1,021m2, this state-of-the-art space has been acoustically modelled to have the properties of the world's finest concert halls. The building provides rehearsal and recording facilities to the Irish Chamber Orchestra and includes soloist and conductor rehearsal rooms, an instrument store room, a musicians's common room and spacious office accommodation for the orchestra's administrative team.
