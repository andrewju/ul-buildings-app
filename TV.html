Thomond Village

Home Location Like

Thomond Village is located on the north campus, which is linked to the south campus across the River Shannon by a vehicular bridge and a spectacular pedestrian bridge - the Living Bridge. Thomond Village offers 2/4/6-bedroom apartments with stunning views of the River Shannon and the Errina Canal. All bedrooms offer en suite bathrooms. While Thomond Village has its own launderette, it shares communal facilities with neighbouring Dromroe Village. As students pass through Dromroe en route to Thomond Village, they can avail of the convenience shop and village hall. The hall and seminar rooms can be used for meetings and study groups.
