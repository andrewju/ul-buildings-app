International Business Centre

Home Location Like

The IBC is currently home to the Johnson & Johnson Development Centre as part of the UL Enterprise Corridor. The facility is adjacent to a number of key research and innovation facilities at UL, including Lero (the Irish Software Engineering Research Centre) and the Nexus Innovation Centre. The Education Procurement Service (EPS), which provides a shared procurement service for higher education in Ireland, and UniJobs, a recruiting agency, are also based in the IBC.
