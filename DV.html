Dromroe Village

Home Location Like

Dromroe Village is an attractive apartment-style complex, bordering the Mill stream and overlooking the River Shannon. The village apartments sleep 6 people; all bedrooms are large and include en suite facilities. It is ideal for all students, particularly senior students who want more space and a serious study environment. Dromroe Village also offers rooms for students with impaired mobility and apartments suitable for families.
