General Information

The Health Sciences Building was completed in 2005 by BDP Architects. The main disciplines are nursing and clinical therapies. With an area of 6,186m2, the Health Sciences Building is the first that the University constructed on the north bank of the campus. It houses the departments of Clinical Therapies (Occupational Therapy, Speech & Language Therapy and Physiotherapy) and Nursing & Midwifery as well as 2 large laboratories for the Department of Life Sciences. The building contains a lecture theatre, 8 classrooms and a restaurant.
