Tierney Building

Home Location Like

The Tierney Building was completed in 2011. With an area of 3,647m2, the building houses the Nexus Innovation Centre and Lero - the Irish Software Engineering Research Centre. The Nexus Innovation Centre is the latest Enterprise Ireland campus incubation centre and supports the formation and growth of 35 new high-potential start-up companies.
